Mission and Vision

We believe Good Gums can fill customers' own space, time, and hearts through plants.

There are people who worry about a better lifestyle for a true "me." GoodGums solves market problems and customers' problems so that space, people, and plants can get closer and make better life changes.

It's easy for people who don't know much about plants to share joys and sorrows with plants. Our motto is to enjoy the entire process, solve each other's problems, empathize, and grow together.

History

Australia's garden industry has existed since the pioneering era. Shortly after the Australian Federation, in the early 1900s, Australia's Nurserymen officially began to establish various groups that exist nationwide.

Our Good Gums company was also established in 2005 to protect the Australian environment and meet the needs of our customers. Our experts are constantly working and communicating with our customers in order to create a good variety. We promise to always provide the best service to our customers.
